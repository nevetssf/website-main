I've been scanning old photographs and I can't remember where most of them came from. The Old World wasn't very good at remembering.

Obama met with local CEOs. CEOs are the representatives of the shareholders to the boards — their primary function is talking. They understand technology to the extent that it increases share prices.

A brief evening with corporate leaders cannot produce meaningful policy. If you want to understand what's actually happening, you have to visit the work floors and dedicate real time to genuine engagement.
