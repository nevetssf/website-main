Title: Reclaiming My Blog
Date: 2026-03-01
Slug: reclaiming-blog
Status: published

So, after many years, and getting increasingly confused about the future of Wordpress, and perplexed by the Wordpress environment in general, I've reclaimed my blog by using `pelican`, storing the raw material in a repo on github, and publishing the static HTML directly to my host. Now posts are just simple Markdown, and if I get tired of my host, it's super simple to move to a new one.

I've moved some of my old posts to the new site. My Wordpress go so infected I lost chunks big chunks of text, like some bacteria eating the flesh of my postings. But they weren't a great loss. I'll make new ones. 

Lots going on in the world. Is there ever a time there doens't feel like there's a lot going on in the world?

One big one is generative AI, in particular LLM tools like the Claude family of tools. It's going to a be a wild ride.
