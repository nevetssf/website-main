I just got an iPhone 4. It's about 20x faster than the iPhone 3G running iOS4.

If I were Apple, I'd have purposely crippled the iPhone 3G to force people into paying hard cash for upgraded hardware. But I'm not Apple.

I don't care about the antenna issues other people have been having because I always wear a white glove when using Apple products. It protects all of us.

As an aside, although it works much better than my last phone, I'm heartened that it really is just a phone.
