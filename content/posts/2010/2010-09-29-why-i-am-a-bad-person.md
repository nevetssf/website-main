Title: Why I am a bad person
Date: 2010-09-29
Slug: why-i-am-a-bad-person
Status: published

On the back stairs of our house a spider built a very impressive Web a few days ago. It's a big spider – probably 1.5 cm in diameter – and it likes to sit in the middle of the Web. The other day my partner and I were taking laundry down and noticed the Web. Impressed – because it was an impressive Web – we contorted ourselves to avoid damaging it or hurting the spider. It was difficult. I felt it was the right thing to do.

This morning our neighbor's workers partially blocked our driveway and my only thought was 'Tow, tow, tow!'

Coincidentally, I walked through the spider's Web on my way to the car a few minutes before.
