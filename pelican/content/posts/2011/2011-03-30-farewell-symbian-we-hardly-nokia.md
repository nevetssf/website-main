Title: Farewell Symbian, we hardly Nokia!
Date: 2011-03-30
Slug: farewell-symbian-we-hardly-nokia
Status: published

I still have a Symbian phone, at least I think I do. It's a back up, in case the end times aren't in my favor. This grand temple of technology we've been creating, fueled by greed and benevolence, is coming to roost in the form of always-connected shiny stuff.

So, Microsoft's strategy is to back up a big truck of money and dump it on some Finnish lady's lawn. OK, I get that.

Still, Lady Macbeth had her dreams, and you've broken her heart!
