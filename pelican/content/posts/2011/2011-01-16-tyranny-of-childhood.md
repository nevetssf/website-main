Title: Tyranny of Childhood
Date: 2011-01-16
Slug: tyranny-of-childhood
Status: published

Once, I was a child, but I'm not a child anymore. Death is relentless, but children shouldn't know that. Let them construct death their right way.

This morning I learned that my high school German teacher died a few weeks ago. She had a profound influence on my world outlook. Austrian, with a harsh interpretation of what was true and false, she left an imprint I'm still trying to decipher. In another world she'd have been a movie star, but I hope in her world she did become a star. She was a devoted teacher and I respected her.

This world is too random to warrant a lot of analysis.
