Title: What's wrong with Windows 8?
Date: 2012-12-07
Slug: whats-wrong-with-windows-8
Status: published

There is some good here. I like the more baked-in encryption, I like the quasi-embedded SkyDrive – which is much less expensive than other options. I like the desktop environment.

So, why did Microsoft shove full-screen apps down our throats? Don't we all have > 27″ non-touch monitors these days? So what am I supposed to do with an e-mail app with 48 point fonts? And no ability to flag anything?

Its as if two committees got together and did heroin and enjoyed it, so they decided to get married. You need to make choices – staying high is not a choice!

I'm all in with Windows 8, and that's my review of Windows 8.
