I woke up at 3:30 AM and read Kathy Griffin's book *For Your Consideration*. She has some meat to her. She spent about a dozen years scrapping for recognition before eventually getting it — inspiring, to go for a goal and eventually to get it. Benjamin Franklin had a similar quality.

There's real value in forgotten autobiographies. They're one of the gifts of the eBook tidal wave.
