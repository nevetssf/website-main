I work at a fabless GPU semiconductor company in Santa Clara on yield-related projects. My specialty is failure analysis tools and techniques, with a particular focus on optical failure analysis for integrated circuit yield improvement.

Most of my post-graduate career has been spent developing tools for failure analysis. Engineering and marketing are inextricably linked in this work — why fight it, do both.

Beyond my primary work, I have professional and semi-professional interests in photography and large-scale image manipulation. I experimented with producing photographic black-and-white prints from color printers using custom grey ink systems long before Epson released the 3800.

I'm an early adopter with a persistent interest in gadgets and new technology in general. It hasn't yielded the material advantages one might expect, but I remain optimistic.
